# Shaft and Bearing Selection Study

## Project

Egg Grading Machine (EGM-01)

---

# Purpose

This study evaluates shaft dimensions, shaft material, bearing type, and mounting methods for the conveyor drive system.

---

# Shaft Selection

Selected Diameter

10 mm

Selected Material

Mild Steel

Reason

Provides sufficient strength and rigidity while remaining economical and easy to machine.

---

# Bearing Selection

Selected Type

Pillow Block Bearing

Reason

Provides simple installation, excellent shaft alignment, and easy maintenance.

---

# Sprocket Mounting

Selected Method

Set-screw mounting with thread-locking compound.

---

# Chain Tensioning

Selected Method

Adjustable bearing mounting slots.

---

# Final Engineering Decision

## Shaft

10 mm Mild Steel

## Bearing

10 mm Pillow Block Bearing

## Mounting

Set Screw

## Chain Tension

Adjustable Bearing Mount
