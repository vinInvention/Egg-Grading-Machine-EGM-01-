# Existing Egg Graders Research

# Project

Egg Grading Machine (EGM-01)

---

# Purpose

The purpose of this study is to investigate existing egg grading machines, identify their strengths and weaknesses, and extract design ideas that can improve EGM-01.

---

# Industrial Egg Graders

Characteristics

- High speed
- Multiple conveyor lanes
- Computer controlled
- Automatic weighing
- Automatic sorting
- Crack detection
- Barcode systems
- Automatic packing

Advantages

- Extremely fast
- Highly accurate
- Reliable

Disadvantages

- Very expensive
- Large footprint
- High maintenance cost
- Requires skilled operators

Suitability

Not suitable for small and medium poultry farms.

---

# Semi-Automatic Egg Graders

Characteristics

- Manual loading
- Automatic weighing
- Automatic sorting

Advantages

- Lower cost
- Easier maintenance
- Lower power consumption

Disadvantages

- Lower throughput
- Some manual labour required

Suitability

Highly suitable for the EGM-01 project.

---

# Manual Egg Grading

Characteristics

- Human judgement
- Manual weighing
- Manual sorting

Advantages

- Very low initial cost

Disadvantages

- Slow
- Labour intensive
- High egg breakage
- Inconsistent grading

Suitability

Current client process.

---

# Engineering Observations

Industrial egg graders demonstrate excellent throughput but are financially inaccessible to most small and medium poultry farms.

Semi-automatic grading systems provide a practical compromise between automation, affordability, and ease of maintenance.

---

# Design Decisions for EGM-01

Based on this research:

- Manual egg loading will be retained.
- Weight-based grading will be implemented.
- Automatic sorting will be included.
- Industrial features such as crack detection and automatic packing are excluded from Version 1.
- The machine will prioritize affordability, reliability, and ease of maintenance.

---

# Conclusion

The design philosophy of EGM-01 shall follow the semi-automatic egg grading approach, providing an affordable and practical solution for small and medium-scale poultry farms.
