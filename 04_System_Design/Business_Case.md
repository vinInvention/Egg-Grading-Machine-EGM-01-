# Business Case

## Project

Egg Grading Machine (EGM-01)

---

# Executive Summary

The Egg Grading Machine (EGM-01) is being developed to improve the egg grading process for small and medium-scale poultry farms. The project addresses operational inefficiencies caused by manual grading, including high labour dependence, inconsistent productivity, and egg breakage.

The proposed solution aims to automate the grading process using a chain-driven conveyor, precision weight measurement, and automated sorting mechanisms while remaining affordable for local poultry farmers.

---

# Business Problem

The client currently grades approximately **350 eggs per day** using manual labour.

Current challenges include:

- High dependence on workers.
- Inconsistent grading speed.
- Frequent egg breakage.
- Productivity losses due to worker fatigue and negligence.
- Time-consuming grading process.

These challenges reduce operational efficiency and increase production costs.

---

# Proposed Solution

Develop a semi-automated egg grading machine capable of:

- Automatically transporting eggs.
- Measuring egg weight accurately.
- Sorting eggs into weight categories.
- Reducing manual handling.
- Recording grading statistics.
