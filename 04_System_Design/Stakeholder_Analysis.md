# Stakeholder Analysis

## Project

Egg Grading Machine (EGM-01)

---

## Purpose

This document identifies all individuals and groups who influence, use, maintain, or benefit from the Egg Grading Machine (EGM-01). Understanding stakeholder needs ensures that the system is designed to meet real operational requirements rather than only technical objectives.

---

# Primary Stakeholders

## 1. Client

Name:
Mrs. B

Role:
Farm Owner

Responsibilities:

- Owns the poultry farm.
- Provides project requirements.
- Evaluates the completed machine.
- Approves the final solution.

Needs:

- Reduce labour costs.
- Reduce egg breakage.
- Improve grading efficiency.
- Improve productivity.
- Obtain a reliable and affordable machine.

Importance:
Very High

---

## 2. Machine Operator

Role:

Farm worker responsible for operating the machine.

Responsibilities:

- Load eggs.
- Start grading process.
- Empty collection trays.
- Monitor operation.

Needs:

- Easy operation.
- Minimal training.
- Safe working environment.
- Simple controls.

Importance:
Very High

---

## 3. Maintenance Technician

Role:

Responsible for servicing the machine.

Responsibilities:

- Lubricate moving parts.
- Replace worn components.
- Calibrate load cell.
- Repair faults.

Needs:

- Easy access to components.
- Clear documentation.
- Standard replacement parts.

Importance:
High

---

## 4. Project Engineer

Name:

Mr. Chinonyerem Vincent

Responsibilities:

- Design system.
- Select components.
- Build prototype.
- Test system.
- Deliver final machine.

Needs:

- Reliable components.
- Accurate documentation.
- Successful project delivery.

Importance:
Very High

Needs:

- Reduced damaged eggs.
- Better product quality.

Importance:

Medium

---

## Farm Management

Needs:

- Increased productivity.
- Reduced operating costs.
- Accurate grading statistics.

Importance:

High

Medium

## Conclusion

The success of EGM-01 depends on satisfying the needs of its primary stakeholders, particularly the client and machine operator. Design decisions throughout the project shall prioritize ease of use, reliability, safety, maintainability, and operational efficiency.
