# Final Engineering Decision

## Selected Option

Chain conveyor with individual egg holders.

## Reason

The chain-and-holder system provides positive mechanical drive, controlled egg positioning, and repeatable movement to the weighing station. These characteristics are important because EGM-01 must measure individual eggs and reliably position them for weighing and sorting.

## Advantages

- Accurate positioning.
- Low risk of drive slippage.
- Suitable for indexing.
- Individual egg control.
- Compatible with stepper motor control.
- Can be fabricated using locally available components.

## Disadvantages

- Requires accurate mechanical alignment.
- Chain tension must be maintained.
- Requires periodic maintenance.
- Poor holder design could increase egg breakage.

## Future Considerations

Future versions may investigate:

- Timing belt transport.
- Modular plastic conveyor chains.
- Multi-lane transport.
- Continuous-motion weighing systems.
